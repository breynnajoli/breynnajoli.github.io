Hi, I’m Breyn.

I am a Control and Instrumentation Engineer with a passion for bringing physical systems into the digital world.

I specialize in bridging the gap between factory-floor hardware, software development, and cloud infrastructure. With a strong foundation in industrial automation, I have hands-on experience designing and troubleshooting control systems. I am highly proficient in configuring Human-Machine Interfaces (HMIs) and Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA) systems, specifically utilizing Wonderware InTouch to monitor, visualize, and optimize real-time production data.

Beyond the factory floor, I am an AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner and an active programmer. I enjoy building practical software tools—such as custom C++ console applications and localized financial calculators—to solve complex, real-world logic problems. 

Whether I am calibrating sensors on a processing line, developing new C++ software features, or configuring secure cloud architectures, my goal is to make complex systems run seamlessly and efficiently. When I am not working with PLCs or writing code, I am usually exploring the latest trends in AI, network traffic analysis, and cybersecurity.
